Naming your server
==================

> Feel free to contribute.

## Formats
- `{country}-{city}{clusterNr}-{serverId}`, e. g. `de-fra1-1234`
- `{serverId}.{location}.{country}.<YOUR-DOMAIN>`, e. g. `loadbal1.fra1.de.example.com`
- `{serverId}.infra.<YOUR-DOMAIN>`, e. g. `db12.infra.example.net`
